Music Compositions

  • Confrontation

    An original composition for trumpet, piano, bass and drums. Mixed and mastered in Logic Pro X.

  • The Day We Met

    An original piece for piano trio. Young Women's Composition Competition 2021 3rd Place Winner.

  • Lotus Cascade

    Wind Quintet utilizing the oriental scale. 1st place winner in the 2024 Illinois Music Educators' Association (ILMEA) Small Instrumental Ensemble Category. To be performed by the Sapphire Wind Quintet.

  • Vagabond

    Energetic electronic piece inspired by J-pop. 2nd place winner in the 2024 ILMEA EDM category.

  • Reverberations in 31-EDO

    Microtonal Compositions made using programming language SuperCollider. Honorable Mention in the 2024 ILMEA avant-garde category.

  • Covert Rendezvous

    2023 Chicago Area of Music Teachers' Association Compositions Festival Winner. Written for Big Band.

  • Farewell

    Original piece for piano, vibraphone, and strings. Inspired by the movie "5 Centimeters Apart".

  • Static

    An original song written and sung by me. Recorded at Butcher Boy Studios.
