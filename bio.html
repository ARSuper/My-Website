Bio

Born and raised in Evanston, Illinois, pianist, arranger/transcriber, and self-taught composer Anika Radhakrishnan blends the line between classical, jazz, avant-garde, and everything else that came before, between, and after. Now she's majoring in CS + Music at UIUC and pursuing a career in music software and hardware development to better the lives of her fellow musicians and composers.
